About

For almost 20 years, Aria has been a pioneer and innovates in micro and small agricultural methanization in France.We support you in your project, from design to completion. Our long experience in biogas allows us to offer you efficient, sustainable solutions adapted to your operation and your needs.Aria is today the methanization department of InnoVent, a company developing wind and solar renewable energies in France and internationally.

Technologies

All agricultural effluents methanize. It is a natural process of degradation of organic matter in the absence of oxygen which produces biogas(about 60% methane and 40% carbon dioxide)as well as the digestate, organic effluent which constitutes a high quality organic fertilization which can be substituted for chemical fertilizers

Prestations

Free pre-diagnosis

We suggest that you do a pre-diagnosis of the methanogenic potential of your effluents free of charge. Several scenarios will be studied depending on the possibilities

Pre-Project Summary

Before the APS, the technical, economic and legislative feasibility of the project is clarified. Several scenarios will be studied according to the possibilities of the exploitation, and the APS will make it possible to determine the most suitable solution.

Pre-Project detailed

This is a detailed study of the anaerobic digestion unit to be set up: sizing and construction study, precise costing, administrative procedures.

Project Management assistance

The APM consists in the realization of the execution plans, the diagrams of principle, the choice of the intervening companies and the follow-up of site.
